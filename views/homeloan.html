Documents for Home Loans

  • Proof of residence- Bank Acct Statement / Electricity Bill / Telephone Bill / LL agreement / Employers letter
  • Identity proof- Passport / Pan Card / Photo Credit Card / Driving License /Voters ID
  • Last 6 months bank Statement
  • 2 years ITR/Form 16
  • Latest Passport size Photos
  • 3 months Salary Slips/ Balance sheet
  • 2 Passport Size Photographs

Same set of documents for Co-Applicants.

Tips for getting Home Loan Approval

  • Plan at least 6 months in advance and get your CIBIL score and check for any anomalies and get it resolved. http://www.cibil.com
  • Ensure that checks issued towards any other EMI is not dishonored
  • Do not apply for loans for multiple loans viz. Home loan, car loan, personal loan etc in a short span of time.
  • Do not keep credit card payments outstanding
